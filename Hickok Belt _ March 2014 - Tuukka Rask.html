Tuukka Rask
IS THE
March 2014
MONTHLY WINNER

Tuukka Rask: NHL - Boston Bruins

  • Averaged 2.04 goals against per game - second among goaltenders  that played in 40 games or more.
  • Ranked first in overall save percentage (.930) and even-strength save percentage (.942).
  • Led all goaltenders in the NHL with seven shutouts.
